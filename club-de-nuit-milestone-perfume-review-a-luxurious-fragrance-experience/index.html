When it comes to perfume, the composition is what sets a fragrance apart from others. The combination of top, middle, and base notes creates a unique experience that evolves over time as the perfume interacts with your skin. Let's break down the scent profile of Club de Nuit Milestone.

Top Notes

The top notes are the first impression of the fragrance, often being the most volatile and evaporating relatively quickly. Club de Nuit Milestone opens with a burst of citrus notes, including lemon, bergamot, and a hint of grapefruit. These notes immediately create a fresh and invigorating scent, which is perfect for daytime wear, particularly in warmer weather. The citrus opening is sharp but not overpowering, giving it a clean and energetic feel.

Heart (Middle) Notes

As the fragrance begins to settle, the middle notes emerge. For Milestone, these consist of floral and spicy elements. The most prominent note here is rose, which adds a touch of elegance and depth to the fragrance. Rose can sometimes be overwhelming in perfumes, but here it is delicately blended with hints of geranium and jasmine. These floral notes soften the citrus opening, creating a more complex and balanced scent.

In addition to the florals, there's also a touch of spicy warmth in the heart of the fragrance. While not dominant, this spicy element adds intrigue and sophistication, making the perfume feel both modern and timeless.
