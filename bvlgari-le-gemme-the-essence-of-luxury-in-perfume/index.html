Inspired by the deep blue of lapis lazuli, Le Gemme Lapis is a fragrance that embodies elegance, power, and wisdom. Coralia draws inspiration from the coral gemstone, evoking the energy and vibrancy of the Mediterranean. The fragrance is an alluring blend of citrus, floral, and fruity notes, designed to transport the wearer to a sun-drenched paradise.
